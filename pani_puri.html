Pani Puri

Panipuri is deep-fried breaded sphere filled with potato, onion, or chickpea. It is a common street food in the Indian subcontinent. It is often spiced with tamarind chutney, chili powder, or chaat masala. A Bengali variant, fuchka, uses spiced mashed potatoes as the filling.

Wheat being a staple in the Northern parts of India, rotis, chapatis, and phulkas are the mainstay.

Calories

One serving of pani puri contains 329 calories:


  • 207 calories from carbohydrates
  • 38 calories from proteins
  • 82 calories from fat

The calorie count per serving (6 puris) ranges from 180 to 215 calories. The calories in this snack come from the deep-fried dough, oil, and potatoes.
