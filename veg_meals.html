Dal And Rice

Dal chawal is a combination of steamed rice and cooked dal. It contains carbohydrates, protein, and plant fiber. Dal contains proteins, fibers, vitamins, calcium, and carbs. Rice is a gut-friendly grain. Adding ghee to dal chawal makes it a balanced diet

Calories

A medium-sized cup of dal-rice contains 290 calories and 1.3 grams of protein. A serving of dal chawal has more than 300 calories.

Here are some calorie counts for different types of dal and rice:

  • Dal-rice: 290 calories per medium-sized cup
  • Dal chawal: 146 calories per 100 grams, 293 calories per plate
  • Dal chawal basmati rice with lentils curry: 380 calories per cup
  • Chana dal aur gur chawal: 405 calories per serving

A poori provides about 5 percent of the total daily calorie requirement of a standard adult diet of 2,000 calories
The calorie breakdown of 100 grams of fried whole wheat poori is: 28% fat, 62% carbs, 10% prot
