Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases it is prepared without any meat, substituting vegetables for the meat

Calories

The number of calories in a plate of biryani can vary depending on the ingredients:

  • Paneer biryani: 153 calories per serving
  • Vegetable biryani: 198 calories per serving
  • Egg biryani: 222 calories per serving
  • Chicken biryani: 360 calories per 500g
  • Iraqi biryani: 368 calories per cup

A regular-sized bowl of chicken biryani can contain anywhere from 300-800
Here are some other biryani calorie counts:
  • 100g of chicken biryani has around 150 to 250 calories
  • 100g of biryani has 163 calories
  • 500g of Sainsbury's Indian Vegetable Biryani has 14.2g of protein, 78.9g of carbs, and 23.5g of fat
  • 450g of Co Op Indian Chicken Biryani & Rice has 29.7g of protein, 54g of carbs, and 24.8g of fat
